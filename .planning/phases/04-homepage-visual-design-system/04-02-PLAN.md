Section order rationale:
- Hero (above fold, first impression)
- Pillars (what Kenexis does — core value prop)
- Stats (social proof, builds trust)
- Featured Content (recent activity, shows company is active)
- Newsletter (engagement, lower commitment than contact)
- Contact CTA (strong close, just above footer)

This creates an alternating visual rhythm:
- Hero: dark (navy with image overlay)
- Pillars: light (bg-background)
- Stats: dark (bg-navy-900)
- Featured Content: light (bg-muted/50)
- Newsletter: light (bg-background)
